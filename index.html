Realistic models make up 20% of all my models. Realistic models can be both simple and complex. For example, what does it cost to make a model of an iPhone? It turns out to be similar to a low poly phone, only with more details. An inexperienced modeler might even say that it is low poly, but it is not! For example, my model tecno spark 20 is realistic and detailed. It looks like low poly, but in fact it is a light high poly model. Low poly is not exactly squares and triangles. Basically, it is polygonal spheres or, as I said, early cubes. Thus, we understand that most game models are low poly models.
